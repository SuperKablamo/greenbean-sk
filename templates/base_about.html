About Us

Greenbean.me is a leader board service that ranks people and communities on their green status. Sign up using your existing Facebook or Twitter account and start logging all the "green" things you do, and earn votes from friends and neighbors.


This Is How It Works

  • Sign in using your existing Facebook or Twitter account.
  • Do something green and then update your status, like, "I rode my bike to work."
  • Your status updates will be broadcast to your social network(s) of choice.
  • Friends and followers vote on your status updates.
  • You earn points and increase your rank.


Everyday is Earth Day

For each status update, Greenbean users can vote on that status once. You earn more votes by updating your status regularly (being green daily).


Is Your Town The Greenest Of Them All?

Greenbean also ranks votes by location, so enlist your neighbors and prove that your community is greenest of them all!
