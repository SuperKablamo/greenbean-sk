About Us

Greenbean.me is a leader board service that ranks people and communities on their green activities. Sign up using your existing Facebook or Twitter account and start logging all the "green" things you do, and earn votes (greenbeans) from friends and neighbors.


This Is How It Works

  • Sign in using your existing Facebook account.
  • Do something green and update your status, like, "I rode my bike to work."
  • Share your green updates with your friends on Facebook.
  • Friends vote on your status updates.
  • You earn points and increase the ranking of you and your neighborhood.


Everyday is Earth Day

For each status update, Greenbean users can vote on that status once. You earn more votes by updating your status regularly (being green daily).


Is Your Town The Greenest Of Them All?

Greenbean also ranks votes by location, so enlist your neighbors and prove that your community is greenest of them all!

What's The Point?

To promote sustainable activities, encourage friends and neighbors to go green, and recognize all the small but important daily efforts of people around the world to preserve the earth - we've only got one!
